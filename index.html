Bio

I have been writing and performing music for over 16 years. Recently I have been performing in the Bay Area, organizing jam sessions with fellow musicians, taking classes at Women’s Audio Mission to learn sound engineering, and recording some of my new music. My lyrics are often darkly witty, with a strong melody and an indie folk-rock style. I am eager to stretch my musical knowledge to experiment with new techniques. I’d like to bring my work with sound and code together.

I have been fortunate to get to enough to produce two shows that I wrote. I wrote the music and libretto for “Love and a Lion,” an opera for 8 singers, cello, violin and keyboard, and produced it in 2008 at my school and in the Minnesota Fringe Festival. I wrote the script and music for “No Catharsis”, a musical with two actors, vocals, xylophone, keyboard and guitar, and produced the show in the 2013 Minnesota Fringe Festival. My theater work also includes designing and building costumes, designing and building sets, designing lighting, acting, dancing and running box office.

About Me

This is the portfolio website for Mathilde Mouw-Rao. My artwork spans music, code, and the visual arts. As a singer-songwriter, I write and perform under the name Tilde Mouw. I have also written and produced an opera, Love and a Lion (2008), and a musical, No Catharsis (2013).

I work as a full-time software engineer writing code in Ruby, Javascript, HTML and CSS, and using the Rails and Angular frameworks.
